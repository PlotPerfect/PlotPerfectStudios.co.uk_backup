What is SEO and Why Does It Matter?

Search engine optimization (SEO) is the process of improving your website’s visibility in search engines like Google. Effective SEO helps your business attract more qualified visitors, generate leads, and increase revenue—without relying solely on paid ads.

  • Appear higher in Google search results for keywords that matter to your business
  • Drive organic (unpaid) traffic to your website
  • Build trust and credibility with your audience
  • Outperform your competitors online
