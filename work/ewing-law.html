OUR PROCESS

How we approached the redesign

Legal sites must be calm, credible, and clear. This build focused on premium design with structure that supports ranking and conversions.

1) Intent mapping

Identified the services clients search for and structured pages to match high-intent queries.

2) Premium UI direction

Clean typography, controlled spacing, and a refined look built for trust and clarity.

3) Conversion flow

Designed each page to reduce hesitation and guide users into a confident enquiry.

4) Performance + SEO foundations

Optimised structure and load performance to support Core Web Vitals and crawlability.

5) Scalability

Built the layout to expand with more services, FAQs, team pages, and future content without a rebuild.
